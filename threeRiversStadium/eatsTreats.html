Eats & Treats

Eats

Headwaters River Pub is located inside Three Rivers Stadium at Gate C. It is open for all Pirates and Steelers games when gates open, throughout the game and after the game as business dictates. There are two full-service bars featuring call and top shelf liquor, microbrew bottles and drafts as well as domestic bottles and draft. ARAMARK Corporation provides food service in the form of a concession area serving upscale stadium fare. Headwaters Pub also features wings from Quaker Steak and Lube in Sharon, PA. Headwaters Pub has a DMX Sound System, Video Wall and approximately 30 TV monitors throughout the facility. Headwaters' capacity is 800 with additional seating outside on the patio at Gate C with a view of downtown Pittsburgh. After each Saturday night Pirates game, Headwaters Pub, in conjunction with WDVE, WTAE-TV and the Pirates, features live music outside on the Patio by local bands. The Pirates and Steelers team store Signature Sports is located at Gate C at Three Rivers Stadium. Signature Sports is open during Pirates and Steelers games from when gates open, throughout the game and after as business dictates. Signature Sports offers the best in official MLB and NFL licensed apparel and merchandise as well as brands such as Nike™ and Starter™. Signature Sports offers specialty items as well as apparel for all ages, sizes and tastes not usually found in sporting goods stores.
