Stadium Tours

General Information

  • Tour Season is April through October.

  • This is a walking tour that takes approximately 1 hour.

  • Tours are scheduled by reservation only Monday thru Friday between 9:00 a.m. and 1 p.m.

  • Origination point for all tours is Gate "B" of Three Rivers Stadium. Participants are to arrive at the time reserved.

  • Free ample parking is available on the roadway under Gate "B".
