Location

From the North

Travel south on I-279 and use exit 12 [Three Rivers Stadium] which then merges onto Reedsdale Street. The left lane leads to Lot #5.

From the South

Travel north on I-279 towards Pittsburgh. Go through the Fort Pitt Tunnels and across the Fort Pitt Bridge. The left lane leads to the Fort Duquesne Bridge. Cross the Fort Duquesne Bridge and the two left lanes lead to Lots 2 and 3.

From the East

Travel west on I-376, Parkway East to the end and follow the ramp to cross the Fort Duquesne Bridge. The two left lanes lead to Lots 2 and 3.

From the West

Travel east on Route 65 to Beaver Avenue near the West End Bridge. Follow the signs to Reedsdale Street. This leads to Lot #5.
